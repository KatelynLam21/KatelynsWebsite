Magic 8 Ball PLTW Project 1.1.9

Ethan and I make a magic 8 ball. The center flashes different colors to signify it's thinking. It generates a random response when asked a question.


Turtle Fish Game PLTW Project 1.2.5

Maddy and I make an interactive turtle fish game. A user controlled turtle moves across the screen to eat a fish which regenerates. The user tries to obtain the highest score of caught fish as possible.


Singing Hippo Valentine's Day Card PLTW Project 1.3.1

Kateryna, Kathy, Sumehra and I make a Hippo Valentine's day card. The user can select the song they want, and they are prompted to enter the recipient's name to be displayed on the card. Finally, the hippo's head will alternate from open mouth to closed mouth to indicate it is singing. A Valentine's day message will also be displayed on the screen.
